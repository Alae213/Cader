## What It Is

Cader is a B2B2C SaaS platform that lets Algerian creators launch paid or free communities,
build structured courses, and get paid directly in Algerian Dinars via CIB or Edahabia bank
cards. For learners, it is the first place to pay for knowledge with a local card, take
courses from Algerian creators, and connect with peers in their Wilaya.

---

## The Problem

Every community and course platform that exists today — Skool, Teachable, Patreon, Kajabi —
was built for Western payment infrastructure. They require Stripe or PayPal. Neither accepts
Algerian CIB or Edahabia bank cards.

The result:
- Algerian creators with real knowledge and real audiences have no viable way to monetize them
  locally. They either give content away for free, redirect to expensive workarounds
  (international bank accounts, informal cash transfers), or don't build at all.
- Algerian learners have no single place to discover local creators, pay with local cards,
  find peers in their Wilaya, or learn in a community built for the Algerian market.

This is not a product feature gap. It is an infrastructure gap. Cader fills it.

---

## The Solution

Two sides, one platform:

**For Creators (Algerian educators, coaches, entrepreneurs):**
- Launch a paid or free community in minutes
- Set pricing in DZD — monthly, annual, one-time, or free
- Build structured courses with modules, pages, and lesson content (video embeds, PDFs, files)
- Get paid directly into their own Algerian bank account via their own Chargily Pay account
- Manage members, moderate content, track basic analytics (v1.1)

**For Students (Algerian learners):**
- Pay with CIB or Edahabia — no foreign card required
- Take structured courses with progress tracking
- Participate in a community feed (posts, comments, reactions)
- Earn points and level up through the gamification system

**Business model:**
- Platform revenue: 2,000 DZD/month creator subscription
- Creator revenue: flows directly to the creator's own Chargily account — the platform takes
  no cut of community or classroom revenue
- Free tier: 50 lifetime unique members (then locked until creator subscribes)

## The Users

### Creator (Community Admin)
- **Who:** Algerian educator, online coach, or entrepreneur
- **Motivation:** Monetize knowledge for a local audience without currency friction
- **Key needs:** Community creation, course builder, Chargily payment setup, basic analytics
- **Tech comfort:** Moderate — familiar with online tools, not necessarily a developer
- **Device:** Primarily desktop (content creation)

### Student (Community Member)
- **Who:** Algerian learner seeking skill development or niche community access
- **Motivation:** Learn from local creators, pay with local cards, connect with nearby people
- **Key needs:** Easy DZD checkout, course progress tracking, feed participation, Wilaya networking
- **Tech comfort:** Low to moderate
- **Device:** Mobile-first, also uses desktop for coursework

### Role model (important)
There are no platform-level Creator or Student account types. Roles — `admin` and `member` —
exist only at the community level, scoped per community. A user can be an admin of community
A and a member of community B simultaneously.

## Differentiator

| What | Why it matters |
|---|---|
| **Chargily Pay (DZD)** | The only reason this product can exist. No other community platform accepts CIB/Edahabia. |
| **Local-first pricing** | Creator sets price in DZD. Students pay in DZD. Zero currency conversion. |
| **Creator owns payment flow** | Creator's own Chargily account receives funds. Platform charges a separate subscription. No payment lock-in. |
